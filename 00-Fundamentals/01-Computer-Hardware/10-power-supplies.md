# Power Supplies

* Convert **AC power (120v, 240v)** to **DC power**

* Provide required voltages:

  * **3.3v**
  * **5v**
  * **12v**

* Not typically **programmable**

* Proper function is **critical to system stability**

* Help protect against **voltage fluctuations**

* Not a full **surge suppressor**

* Choosing **high-quality** power supplies is important

* Poor power supplies can cause **system damage**

* **More vulnerable systems:**

  * Desktops
  * Server towers
  * Rack systems
  * Standalone systems

* **Less vulnerable:**

  * Laptops (use internal battery during power issues)
